Don't Get Pinned

Description

Made more for the people who really look for a challenge in a game, Don't Get Pinned is a simple, physics-based platformer. The objective is to get to the finish without running into lava or being held in place (pinned) by pinners.

A large part of what makes this game difficult is that you can't jump like in most platformers. Instead, you have a large amount of friction. In order to 'jump' you need to bounce off of another object to gain height.
